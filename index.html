POST OFFICE: SCENARIO FOUR

On this page, you will find a chatbot for customer service in a post office scenario. In this scenario, you are interested in sending some old clothes to a friend through the Post Office. None of the clothes have significant monetary value. In fact, you had planned to donate them to charity, but your friend expressed interest in having them instead. Your priority is ensuring that the postage costs remain as low as possible. You are using the chatbot to find out more information about potential postage options.

Although the information provided by the chatbot is informed by real world sources, the chatbot itself doesn’t have any connections to any actual Post Office services. The addresses of the receiver and sender are also fictitious. The chatbot is only able to respond to queries in English.

You can start with either Task 1 or Task 2. However, ensure that you complete Task 3 last.

Complete the following tasks using the chatbot:

  • Task 1: Ask the chatbot for the Post Office opening times.
  • Task 2: Ask the chatbot for the average delivery time for parcels.
  • Task 3: Ask the chatbot to walk you through postage options available for sending a parcel of clothes.

Note: During Task 3, you will be asked whether you wish to purchase your requested postage option. There is no right or wrong way to proceed – rejecting or accepting the offer will both eventually lead to the end of the simulation – so please answer truthfully with regards to how you feel.

You will NOT be asked to enter any sensitive information.
